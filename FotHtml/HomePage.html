What We Offer

Supporting the creation and management of electronic contracts between traders and transportation companies.

Tracking System

tracking system that allows tracking the progress and location of shipments in real time.

Order Management

Enabling traders to manage their orders. Providing an overview of active orders' details including status and estimated delivery times.

How does it work?

  • Acceptance of the Request and Completion of Transport

    The trader waits for the transport company to review and accept the request. Upon acceptance, the transportation process begins, fulfilling the request and completing the transaction.

  • Searching for the Suitable Transport Company

    After requests are submitted, the system automatically searches and matches the requests with suitable transport companies

  • Submission of Requests by Traders

    Traders register their requests on the platform, where they specify shipment details such as origin, destination, truck type, quantity, weight, value, and desired delivery date.
